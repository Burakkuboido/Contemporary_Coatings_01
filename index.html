Who Are We?

Contemporary Coatings specialise in providing professional painting & decorating services in Kent...

We have been in the industry for more than 35 years combined and have developed a strong reputation built on recommendation.

We receive the majority of our work through repeat business with existing clients and personal recommendation.

We feel this is testimony to the high standard of work we provide!

How can we help you?

Our team consists of professionals who have a wealth of experience and expertise in this line of business. We can offer you personalised services to suit your style and requirements. Our business has been built around the core values of honesty, integrity, professionalism and reliability. We believe in working with our customers to ensure that their expectations are not just met, but exceeded. We are also happy to offer advice and suggestions to ensure that you maximise the potential of your home.
